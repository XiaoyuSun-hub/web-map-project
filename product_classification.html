Land-use Classification of Enschede





Description


The original image that was chosen to make the supervised classification is Sentinel-2A satellite image of Enschede at 25 September2016. 9 spectral bands are used to make the classification, with a resolution from 10m to 20m. Training sample and reference sample are interpreted from the original image. And the pixel-based algorithms of maximum likelihood classification (MLC) and support vector machine (SVM) are used to classify the image. It can then be analyzed for the uncertainty and quality according to the method used and the accuracy report can be generated.
